The Gender Wage Gap

Pay Gap

noun[ C, usually singular ] HR, WORKPLACE UK US (also wage gap); (wage differential)


the difference between the average pay of two different groups of people, for example men and women

The Problem

Women are not only almost half the workforce, they are also the sole breadwinner in half of American families with children. However, on average, women earn less than men in almost every single occupation. According to research done by the IWPR, if change continues at the same pace it has done for the past 50 years, women will reach pay parity in 2059 (40 years).
