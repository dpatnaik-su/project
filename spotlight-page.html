News on the Gender Wage Gap

The gender pay gap will remain until companies change their ways

Natasha Lamb and Jennifer Klein write a commentary piece about how the US Women's National Soccer Team ignited a revolutionary movement for gender wage equality. They urge companies to use their power to create lasting structural change.

Ellevest Co-Founder Sallie Krawcheck Helps Women Invest

Coryanne Hicks writes an article about Sallie Krawcheck's company, Ellevest. Ellevest is a digital investment advisor designed for specifically for women to combat the long history of a male dominated financial industry.

Can Transparency Laws Fix the Gender Wage Gap?

Baker, Halberstam, Kroft, Mas, and Messacar conducted a study to see the effect that the pay transparency legistlation had in the public sector in Canada. They studied administrative data on faculty salaries at public universities across the country.
